Ahmed Mohammed Camara - Administrator         

Ahmed Mohammed Camara is an Administrator with Macauley Institute for research. He is a trained Accountant with years of experience in Administration, Accounting and Management with the Oil and Gas sector, Hospitality and Tourism industry, Professional Consultancy sector, and the Media Industry.

Camara's experience spans in areas of financial administration, human capital management, operations, procurements and logistics management with, K.C. Igboasoiyi and Co. (Chartered Accountants), Leadership Group Newspapers Limited ( Print Media Firm), Gulf Treasures limited (Oil and Gas Firm) and Quarter Jack Metrowheels limited.

Camara holds a BSc. in Accounting, Higher National Diploma in Business Administration and Management and an Advanced Diploma in Civil Law. He is a graduate member of the Nigeria Institute of Management and a professional student member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria and Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria.
